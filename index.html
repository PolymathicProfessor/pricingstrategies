üí° Pricing Strategy Options

Based on COST

  • Cost-Plus Pricing: Add markup to total costs
  • Keystoning: Retailers double the cost (100% markup)

Based on DEMAND

  • Target Costing: Start with price, design backward
  • Yield Management: Different prices for different customers
  • Congestion Pricing: Higher prices during peak demand

Based on COMPETITION

  • Price Leadership: Follow industry leader
  • Status Quo: Keep prices similar to competitors

Based on CUSTOMER NEEDS

  • Value Pricing (EDLP): Everyday low pricing
  • High/Low Pricing: Regular price with promotions
